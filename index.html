FAQ
🟣 Are there any design requests you don't support?
Absolutely. SoftoDesign does not cover the following design work: 3D modeling, animated graphics (GIFS, etc.), document design (medical forms, etc.), complex packaging, and extensive print design (magazines, books, etc.).
🟣 Can I ask for a refund?
Currently SoftoDesign does not offer any Refunds and will not in future. But you can always pause or cancel your subscription.
🟣 What is the turnaround time?
On average, most requests are completed in just 2/3 days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.
🟣How many requests can I have?
Once subscribed, you're able to add as many design requests to your queue as you'd like, and they will be delivered one by one.
🟣 How do I request/ What happens after I Subscribe?
After Subscribed you will receive an welcome e-mail with all the details inside. We will guide you the rest from there.
🟣 When will I get charged?
You will be charged per month the same amount. The same way you bought the subscription.
🟣 What payment methods do you support?
We Support Payoneer, Xoom, Remitly, Direct Bank Transfer, Credit/Debit Card payments.
For specific reasons we don't support PayPal, Stripe or any other online payment processor yet.
🟣 What's Difference between you and a Full-time designer?
Hiring a full-time senior designer can be costly, with annual salaries surpassing $100,000, not to mention additional benefits. Moreover, finding an available candidate can be quite challenging. Another potential issue is the fluctuating workload; there may be periods when there isn’t enough work to justify full-time employment, yet you’re still obligated to pay for their downtime.

Opting for a monthly subscription model offers flexibility. You have the freedom to activate or deactivate your subscription based on your project needs, ensuring that you only incur costs when there’s actual work to be assigned to your designer
🟣 Are we signing any contract?
We are not signing any contract. Its just a simple monthly payment system.
🟣 Working days/Off Days?
We are available the whole week except  "Friday, Saturday". We are not available in National Holiday's. We are also not Available in EID, Ramadan and other Islamic days.
